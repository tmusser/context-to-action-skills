# Post-Meeting Follow-Up Example

## Situation

You have a Zoom transcript and rough notes from a launch meeting. You need a clean follow-up without dumping the raw transcript.

## Input

```text
Use meeting-to-actions on the transcript below.
Then draft a warm follow-up I can send to the launch group with the decisions, owners, due dates, and open blocker.
```

## Useful Output Shape

- Decisions made
- Open decisions
- Action items with owner and due date
- Blockers
- Follow-up draft
- Tickets or docs to update
- Source gaps
